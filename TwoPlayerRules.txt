Two player mode:
The player (Player A) who has the Heart 3 after dealing has the chance to play first.
he can choose to play a single card or a pair or three cards or four cards. 
Multiple cards have to have the same value.
Player B has to choose same number of card(s) but higher value to beat Player A’s card(s). 
This loop continues until one of them cannot beat the card(s) on the table. 
After that, the last player has the chance to start a new round. 
When one of the players has no more hand cards, this player is the winner.
