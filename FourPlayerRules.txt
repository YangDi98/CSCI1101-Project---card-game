Four player mode:
Four players are divided into two groups depending the input order. 
The first and the third are in the same group. 
The second and the fourth are in the same group.
The first player gets to play first, he can choose how many up to four cards to play
Multiple cards have to have the same value.
The next person choose same number of cards(s) but higher value to beat previous card(s).
The first group of two players who have no cards are the winners.
